About Me

Hello! I'm Noah Betancourt, a passionate programmer with a Bachelor of Science in Biomedical Science from Texas A&M University, with a minor in Bioinformatics. My journey in programming and technology has been driven by a keen interest in developing innovative solutions and a dedication to continuous learning.

During my academic tenure, I gained substantial knowledge and hands-on experience in various programming languages including Python, Java, and C++, as well as web development technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am well-versed in using frameworks and tools such as React.js, Spring Framework, and MySQL, which I have applied in multiple projects.

My technical skills are complemented by a strong foundation in bioinformatics and laboratory skills, which I honed through my coursework and research projects. I have also demonstrated leadership and teamwork abilities through various roles in clubs and organizations.

In my most recent role at Dev-10 Academy, I developed a fully functional back-end suite and professional front-end applications using MySQL, JUnit, Spring Boot, React.js, and TypeScript. This experience has equipped me with the skills to produce high-quality, full-stack applications.

Outside of work and academics, I was actively involved in extracurricular activities, including the Aggie Research Club and the 3-D Regeneration in Silico Research team. These experiences have further enriched my knowledge and skills, particularly in the fields of bioinformatics and image processing.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to ask more about my projects and skills. I am always open to new opportunities and collaborations.
